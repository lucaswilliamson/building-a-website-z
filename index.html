Welcome!

Pakistan is a multicultural country that contains a diversity of ethnolinguistic groups. This website contains a summary of the hallmarks of the Pakistani languages, including a brief overview of the history of linguistic development in the region as well as the current geographical distribution of languages.

According to Ethnologue, there are 74 living languages spoken in Pakistan, and there are no Pakistani languages considered extinct. For the sake of brevity, this website will focus most extensively on the historical development and the status of the six most widely spoken languages in the country: Punjabi, Pashto, Sindhi, Saraiki,Balochi and Urdu.
